Business support for a project or opportunity

Get tailored support for a new project or opportunity

If you’ve identified an opportunity or project that could help your business grow and thrive, you may be able to access specialist, one-to-one support from us that’s tailored to your business.

Depending on your needs, you may be able to access:

  • Insights, knowledge and advice from our specialist teams
  • Help focusing your priorities
  • Help developing strategies and make business decisions
  • Help accessing other forms of support from our partners and third parties
  • Support for building capability and resilience
  • New contacts, connections and networks that could help you
In some cases, your tailored support plan could also include some funding.

What kind of opportunities and projects can we support?

We can provide advice and support for projects and opportunities in key business areas, including:

  • Growing your turnover
  • Entering new international markets
  • Developing new products and services
  • Developing new systems and processes
  • Improving productivity
  • Reducing carbon usage and improving environmental performance
  • Implementing new a business model or strategy
  • Making better use of your people and adopting a new organisation structure
  • Making your business more resilient to economic changes

Who can get support?

We want to keep this support open to ask many businesses as possible. We’re mainly interested in what your opportunity is, how it could help you and how it might contribute to building a fairer, greener economy.

To be eligible for support, you’ll need to have already identified a project or opportunity and done your research on how it will help your business, what difficulties you’re facing and what the outcomes might be.

We’re particularly interested in supporting projects that could contribute to:

  • More businesses in Scotland with employees who are paid the real living wage
  • More environmentally responsible businesses who prosper by using less resources
  • Productivity gains to help drive more, better quality jobs that pay higher salaries, provide security of hours and more predicable work pattern
  • More equitable distribution of growth income and wealth across Scotland

Businesses we can’t support

Our support services aren’t available to companies who are involved in the following sectors or activities:

  • Banking, insurance or sub-prime lending
  • Gambling
  • Adult entertainment
  • Tobacco
  • Locally traded services
  • Any activity that could have a negative impact on human rights

How to apply

If you’re interested in receiving specialist advisory support from us for a project or opportunity, you can start by filling out a short form telling us a bit about your project and business.

If we think we may be able to support your project, we’ll get in touch directly to gather some more detailed information to help us make a full assessment.

If your project isn’t suitable for our specialist one-to-one support, we’ll still get back to you to let you know what other support services may be suitable.

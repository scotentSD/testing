We work with companies to achieve sustainable growth, both in terms of quality jobs and turnover, delivering economic benefit and prosperity for Scotland.

We work with Scottish companies to identify and implement opportunities across key areas:

  • International growth
  • Supporting new product and process innovation
  • Reducing carbon emissions
  • Strengthening business resilience.

We offer specialist, impartial advice to help make:

  • Strategic business decisions
  • Build capability and resilience
  • Help to navigate and broker support from a wider group of partners.

Our support is tailored to needs and aligned to delivering the following outcomes:

  • More businesses in Scotland with employees who are paid the real living wage
  • More environmentally responsible businesses who prosper by using less resources
  • Productivity gains to help drive more, better quality jobs that pay higher salaries, provide security of hours and more predicable work pattern
  • More equitable distribution of growth income and wealth across Scotland
